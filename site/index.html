# Why your Touch Bar deserves this

📊5h / weekly limit bars

ccusage-style session blocks with reset time (↻1:42 or ↻14:30 — your pick). Orange at 75%, red at 90%. Auto-learned or custom token limits.

✅Accept / Deny on the bar

Official Claude Code PreToolUse hook → localhost, token-authenticated. Tap ✓/✕/Pass. App closed? Everything falls back to the terminal. Zero risk.

📡Your real Claude quota

Uses the Claude Code login already on your Mac to show the same numbers as /usage — 5-hour & weekly percent with true reset times. No login? Local estimates kick in.

🧡Pixel pets

Clawd (the Claude Code mascot), penguin, dragon or ghost — sprinting at your burn rate. The menu bar icon matches your pet. Chill/Normal/Hyper energy.

🎨5 themes + custom

Midnight, Matrix, Neon Sunset, Ocean, Mono — or roll your own colors with a live Touch Bar preview (works on non-Touch-Bar Macs).

🔒100% local

Reads ~/.claude/projects/**/*.jsonl on your machine. No accounts, no telemetry, no cloud. MIT licensed, ~600 KB.
